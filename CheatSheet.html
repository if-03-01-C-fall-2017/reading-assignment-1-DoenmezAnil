World Wide Web General

What are the most essential technologies that made the World Wide Web such a success?

Thanks to Tim Bernes-Lee who is the main person in this categorie, the creator of HTML and the founder of WWW.

Among the most essential technologies that made the WWW such a success are:
  • The uniform resource locator (URL) and uniform resource identifier (URI)
  • The markup language for creating web pages and web applications HTML(HyperTextMarkUpLanguage)
  • And the application protocol for distributed, collaborative, and hypermedia information systems, HTTP(HyperText Transfer Protocol)
